When did pizza first appear in the Italy?

Italy’s first modern pizza was born in 1780: a thin crust topped with fresh mozzarella, tomato sauce and basil called the Margherita pizza. The story goes that it was created in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy, who did not hide her love for this rustic dish, but so delicious.
